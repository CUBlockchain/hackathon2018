About the Event

We are extremely excited to announce the very first CU Blockchain Hackathon coming to the University of Colorado at Boulderâ€™s Idea Forge this April 14th and 15th! Seeing as the closest collegiate hackathon to the University of Colorado Boulder is 500 miles away, CU Blockchain aims to build off the steam generated from the HackCU hackathon in February. This concept was created by members of a quickly growing campus club to expedite the adoption rate of blockchain technology. The club quickly grew to 100+ members with various guest pitches from some of the biggest projects in the space. Former members of the club have gone on to land major positions at some of the most innovative projects in the space.

Through the CU Blockchain hackathon we hope to offer a variety of internship opportunities to students along with education workshops for hackers of all ages. Modeled after the format of ETHDenver, we will be hosting a variety of highly demanded panels from respected companies in the blockchain ecosystem. Additionally, this hackathon provides a perfect opportunity for companies to recruit the youngest talent from the ground up. HackCU gave light to many talented developers who have gone on to contribute immensely to major companies such as Coinbase, Consensys, and the City of Zion. We aim to foster innovation and creativity through a community of students who are motivated to make a lasting difference in the world, by connecting them with other passionate hackers, brilliant mentors, and the best companies.

This February, more than 600 students arrived in Boulder for 24 hours of teamwork, creativity, and immense determination to produce marketable technology. This April, CU Blockchain will be hosting its first ever annual blockchain hackathon. This event aims to bring together over 300 of the best hackers from across the country to the University of Colorado Boulder inspiring community.
